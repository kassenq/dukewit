The Percentage Project

A nationwide mission to raise awareness for the experiences of women and minorities in technology

What it is

The Percentage Project is a project started at the University of Pennsylvania that has now grown into a nonprofit initiative that utilizes the power of statistics and social media to spread the narratives of students of diverse backgrounds in technology.

Duke WiT's participation

Taking inspiration from UPenn, Duke implemented its own version of The Percentage Project in the spring of 2019. Find our photos here and our methodology here. You can also read our feature in The Chronicle here.

Since then, we have worked collaboratively with UPenn and other universities nationwide every year to get more students involved.
